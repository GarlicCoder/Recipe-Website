Ingredients

Serving:
  • 4 cups of Dried Oats
  • 0.5 cups of Neutral Oil
  • 0.25 cups of Honey
  • 0.75 cups of Sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons Cinnamon
  • 0.25 teaspoons Cinnamon

Prep Time: 10-15 minutes

Bake Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 - 40 minutes

Difficulty:

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Put oil, cinnamon, sugar, honey, and salt into a bowl. Whisk until mixed thouroughly.Taste and adjust if needed.
  3. Add dry oats to mixed bowl.
  4. Lay out granola mixture onto a pan with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes. Mix granola halfway through.
  6. Take out of oven and let rest for 10-15 minutes. The granola will harden over time.
